San Diego's plans to divert landfill waste

repl.it Implementation of the zero waste plan:
  • The City of San Diego plans to fully implement public space recycling at parks, beaches, transit centers, recreation centers, and libraries.
  • It will establish an outreach program that emphasizes the hierarchy of food waste reduction: reduce, reuse/rescue, compost.

San Diego has created an ambitious “zero waste” plan to reduce waste by 75% by 2030.
